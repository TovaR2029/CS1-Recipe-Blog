Description

Strawberries and nutella is an amazing, tasty combo and somewhat healthy. It is also a very good after school snack because you most likely have these items at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of strawberries
  • 1 jar of nutella

Steps

  1. Cut the stem off the strawberries.
  2. Wash & dry them.
  3. Put them into your bowl.
  4. Grad a spoon and your nutella.
  5. Finally, drizzle your nutella on top of the strawberries.
  6. Bon appetit!

Description

This snack combo is a very delicious sweet and salty treat that is very easy to make. It's great for movie nights with your family and especially if it's a scary movie in the fall!

Ingredients

  • A few marshmellows
  • Popcorn

Steps

  1. Grab your popcorn bag.
  2. Microwave your popcorn.
  3. Grab a few marshmellows.
  4. Add it to your popcorn when it is steaming and let it melt.
  5. And your done!

Description

This combo is a very popular one. It is scipy and flavorful, and the cheese makes the ramen very creamy and delicious! This snack is also a dinner for most people which makes it even better, being very filling.

Ingredients

  • Ramen
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Grab a pot.
  2. Make any type of ramen/your usual one.

  4. Wait for it to melt.
