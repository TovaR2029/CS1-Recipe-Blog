Description

Strawberries and nutella is an amazing, tasty combo and somewhat healthy. It is also a very good after school snack because you most likely have these items at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl of strawberries
  • 1 jar of nutella

Steps

  1. Cut the stem off the strawberries.
  2. Wash & dry them.
  3. Put them into your bowl.
  4. Grad a spoon and your nutella.
  5. Finally, drizzle your nutella on top of the strawberries.
  6. Bon appetit!
